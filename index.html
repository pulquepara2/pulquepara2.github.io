Bacalar, the hidden gem at the Riviera Maya

Locally known as Laguna Bacalar, ‘La Laguna de los Siete Colores’ (the 7 color lagoon), it’s a stunning freshwater lake that is so clear it lives up to its nickname. If you didn’t know any better, you would think that this place is an idyllic Caribbean seaside town. “Bacalar, The Maldives of Mexico”

It won’t be long before you see Laguna Bacalar popping up all over your Instagram feed. It’s really that beautiful! We’ve put together this Bacalar Mexico travel guide to answer all your questions and inspire you to add it to your Mexico bucket list! If you’ve been to Cancun, Tulum, or Playa del Carmen and are looking for somewhere new – consider a few days in Bacalar, Mexico!

Weather at Bacalar

The best months to travel are the winter months, after the rain season.

Between November and March, the temperatures are lovely and you get to enjoy many sunny hours!
